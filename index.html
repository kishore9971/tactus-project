System Architecture

How TACTUS Works

A three-layer pipeline — computer vision, wireless communication, and haptic actuation — operating in real time with minimal latency.

01 — Vision

POSE TRACKING

A webcam feeds 192×192 frames into MoveNet SinglePose Lightning, tracking wrist position against a configurable target zone.

02 — Logic

DIRECTION LOGIC

When the wrist exits the target zone, Python identifies the boundary crossed and constructs a directional motor command.

03 — Haptics

HAPTIC FEEDBACK

Commands travel wirelessly over ESP-NOW to the band. The onboard IMU corrects for limb rotation to keep feedback accurate.
